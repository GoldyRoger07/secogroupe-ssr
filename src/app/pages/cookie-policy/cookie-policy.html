We use cookies to improve your experience on the site. Below is a simple explanation:

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help the site remember certain information (preferences, session, etc.).

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: necessary for the site to function.
  • Performance/analytics cookies: to analyze site usage.
  • Functional cookies: to remember your choices (language, preferences).

3. Managing cookies

You can set your browser to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Note that some site features may stop working if you disable essential cookies.
